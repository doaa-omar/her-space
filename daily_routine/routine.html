✨ Beauty Routine

Practical steps for glowing skin & complete wellness

🌸
Skin Care
4 essential daily steps
Face Cleansing
1
Wet your face with lukewarm water
2
Apply a cleanser suited to your skin type and massage in circular motions for 2 minutes
3
Rinse with cold water to tighten pores
4
Pat dry with a soft towel — never rub
>
Moisturizer
1
Apply while skin is still slightly damp
2
Use gentle upward strokes — never pull downward
3
Don't forget your neck and décolletage
4
Morning and night — consistency is key
Sunscreen
1
Use SPF 30 or higher every single day
2
Apply 15–20 minutes before going outside
3
Reapply every 2 hours if you're in the sun
4
Cover ears, neck and the back of your hands too
🛁
Bath Routine
3 steps for soft, hydrated skin
Shower
1
Use warm — not hot — water (hot water dries out skin)
2
Wash from top to bottom
3
Keep showers under 10 minutes
4
Pat dry immediately with a soft towel
Body Scrub
1
Apply on damp skin
2
Once a week is enough
3
Focus on elbows, knees and heels
4
Rinse well and enjoy the instant softness
Body Moisturizer
1
Apply within the first 3 minutes after showering — that's the secret!
2
Massage in to boost circulation
3
Elbows and knees need extra product
4
Body cream is better than lotion for dry skin
💅
Nail Care
3 steps for healthy, elegant nails
Nail Trimming
1
Trim after showering — nails are softer
2
Cut straight, then gently round the edges with a file
3
Never cut the living skin around the nail
4
A glass file is gentler than metal — prevents splitting
Cuticle Moisturizing
1
Apply oil (almond or coconut) daily to your cuticles
2
Gently push cuticles back after showering
3
Never cut them — just push gently
4
Wear gloves when washing dishes or cleaning
Healthy & Elegant Nails
1
Trim your nails regularly every 1–2 weeks
2
Massage with almond or castor oil daily for strength and shine
3
Stay hydrated — inner hydration shows on your nails
4
Biotin (found in eggs & nuts) strengthens nails from the root
💪
Exercise & Health
3 habits that will change your life
Exercise
1
Start with just 20 minutes a day
2
Always warm up 5 min before and cool down 5 min after
3
Choose an activity you enjoy so you'll stick to it
4
Three times a week is enough to start
Hydration
1
Drink a glass of water first thing in the morning
2
Aim for 8 glasses a day (about 2 litres)
3
Warm lemon water in the morning is a natural detox
4
Use a reminder app or keep a bottle by your side
Sleep
1
Keep a consistent sleep schedule — even on weekends
2
A dark, slightly cool room = deeper sleep
3
Stop screens an hour before bed
4
7–8 hours is the ideal goal
🌿
Natural Recipes
3 recipes from your kitchen
Sugar & Lemon Scrub
1
Mix 1 tbsp sugar + 1 tbsp honey + half a lemon
2
Apply to face with gentle circular motions for 2 minutes
3
Rinse with lukewarm water
⚠️ Avoid sun exposure right after — lemon sensitises skin
Coconut Oil
1
Hair: apply to ends or as an overnight mask
2
Body: excellent moisturiser right after showering
3
Nails: apply daily to cuticles
⚠️ Avoid applying to your face if you have oily or acne-prone skin
Honey Mask
1
Raw, unprocessed honey works best
2
Apply to a clean face and leave for 15–20 minutes
3
Rinse with lukewarm water
4
Suitable for all skin types — naturally hydrating & antibacterial
