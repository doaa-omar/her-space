Dry Hair

Dry hair is caused by lack of moisture and needs proper hydration and care.

Causes

  • Lack of natural oils in the scalp
  • Frequent washing with harsh shampoos
  • Excessive heat styling (straighteners, blow dryers)
  • Chemical treatments (dyeing, bleaching)
  • Environmental factors (sun, wind, dry air)
  • Poor nutrition (low vitamins and healthy fats)
  • Not drinking enough water

Treatment

  • Using moisturizing hair masks regularly
  • Reducing heat styling as much as possible
  • Applying leave-in conditioner
  • Drinking enough water daily
  • Eating foods rich in healthy fats and vitamins
  • Trimming hair ends regularly
  • Using sulfate-free shampoos

Best Oils

  • Coconut oil → deep hydration and softness
  • Argan oil → adds shine and smoothness
  • Olive oil → nourishes dry and damaged hair
  • Jojoba oil → balances scalp moisture
  • Almond oil → strengthens and softens hair

Warnings

  • Avoid washing hair too frequently
  • Do not use hot water when washing hair
  • Avoid heat styling tools daily
  • Do not use shampoos with sulfates or alcohol
  • Avoid brushing hair harshly when dry
  • Do not skip conditioner
