Her Space

A Comfortable Space For Every Girl ✨

Project Description

“Her Space” is a website created to reflect girls’ interests, daily routines, and self-care lifestyle in a simple and elegant way.

We wanted the website to feel realistic, comfortable, and relatable, so we focused on topics that many girls care about every day.

The design uses soft feminine colors and an organized layout to create a calm and visually appealing atmosphere.

We used HTML to build the structure of the website and CSS to improve the styling, colors, spacing, and overall user experience.

Through this project, we wanted to highlight the importance of confidence, self-care, and inner beauty.

Website Sections

  • ✨ Daily Routine: Simple healthy habits that help girls organize their day and feel productive.
  • ✨ Body Care: Tips for maintaining body hygiene, softness, and overall self-care.
  • ✨ Skincare: Daily skincare routines and methods for healthy glowing skin.
  • ✨ Style: Fashion inspiration and outfit ideas for different personalities.
  • ✨ Hijab: Different hijab styles and elegant modest fashion ideas.
  • ✨ Hair: Hair care routines and solutions for common hair problems.
  • ✨ Mouth Care: Oral hygiene tips for healthy teeth and fresh breath.
  • ✨ Makeup: Simple makeup ideas and beauty tips for different occasions.
  • ✨ Diet: Healthy eating habits that support both beauty and wellness.
