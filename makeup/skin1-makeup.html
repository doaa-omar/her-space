Makeup Guide for Light Skin

Eye Makeup

Soft shades like champagne, peach, and light brown
enhance fair skin by adding warmth without overpowering the natural tone.

Face Makeup

Fair skin looks radiant with pink blush and
light bronzer, giving a healthy glow while keeping the complexion balanced.

Lip Makeup

Nude, peach, and soft pink lip colors flatter
light skin tones, creating a natural and fresh look.
